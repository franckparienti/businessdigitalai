The three risks

What your legal and compliance teams need to know

US LAW

Cloud Act (2018)

Forces US cloud providers to give US authorities access to any data they hold — even data stored in Europe. Your contracts with US vendors do not override this law. GDPR does not either.

US LAW

Patriot Act

Allows US intelligence agencies to access data from US-based companies without a warrant or notice. Choosing an American AI is an implicit consent to this access.

EU REGULATION

EU AI Act (2024)

Fines of up to 7% of global turnover for non-compliant AI use in high-risk categories. We build your compliance file and keep it current as the regulation evolves.
