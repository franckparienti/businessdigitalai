The engine.

Five components, run by one team, reported in one dashboard. Nothing is subcontracted to a black box.

01 · Targeting

An ideal customer profile, then a real file

A ninety-minute strategy session defines the segments and the ideal customer profile. We then source decision-maker files with direct mobile numbers, enrich them, validate them and deduplicate against your existing pipeline so we never call an account you already own.

02 · Outbound

An automatic dialer and a full-time team

The dialer places the calls and connects an operator only when a human picks up, which removes the dead time that makes ordinary cold calling uneconomic. Our teams sit in France and Madagascar, francophone natively, with local-language operators added for Turkey and the Gulf.

03 · Paid search

Google Ads at European prices

The same enterprise AI keywords cost roughly four times less per click in France and continental Europe than in the United States. The same media budget buys four times the reach. Every inbound lead is called back by the same team within minutes, which is where the highest conversion sits.

04 · Same-day proposals

The call becomes a costed offer

Calls are recorded and transcribed. Each qualified conversation automatically produces not a meeting note but a costed written proposal, built from what the prospect actually said, and sent the same day while the conversation is still warm.

05 · Conversion

Phone, then demo, then room

Phone meetings escalate to video demonstrations, then to physical meetings, then to IA Afterwork, the monthly Paris gathering of around 120 AI decision-makers we co-organise. A dedicated edition can be staged for your brand.

06 · Reporting

A weekly report and a live dashboard

A written report every Friday. A thirty-minute strategy review every month. A dashboard open from day one showing calls placed, connection rate, meetings booked, ad spend, cost per lead, pipeline value and closed revenue. You keep full access to your own ad accounts throughout.

Questions we always get.

Qualification

What counts as a qualified meeting?

Criteria are agreed in writing at kick-off: company size, sector, seniority of the contact, expressed need and timeline. A meeting counts only if the named decision-maker attends. Nothing is counted retroactively or unilaterally.

Transparency

How do I know this is not a black box?

You receive the calling schedule in advance, you keep full access to your own Google Ads accounts, and the dashboard is live from day one. Call recordings and transcripts are available on request, subject to GDPR.

Compliance

How do you handle GDPR?

B2B prospecting on professional contact data, with lawful basis documented, opt-out honoured immediately and suppression lists maintained across campaigns. Consent and data-protection practices are verified before any campaign starts.

Speed

When do the first results land?

First calls within ten working days of signature, first meetings within fourteen. Cold calling is immediate by nature: there is no ramp-up curve to wait through, only a learning curve that improves the numbers month over month.

Territories

Which markets do you cover?

France and francophone Europe from day one. Turkey and the GCC from month two, with local-language operators added to the same infrastructure and the same dashboard. The engine is territory-agnostic; only the staff and the ad copy change.

After ninety days

What happens at day 90?

A full review against the committed volumes and the meeting floor, with the complete dataset. You renew, scale up, or stop. There is no automatic renewal and no lock-in beyond the ninety days.
