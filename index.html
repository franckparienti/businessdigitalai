Why it matters

Three legal risks your current AI vendor won't tell you about

If you use OpenAI, Microsoft Azure, or Google Cloud for AI, you are already exposed.

US LAW

The Cloud Act (2018)

Compels US cloud providers to hand over data to US authorities — regardless of where the data is stored. Your European customer records, financial models, and HR data are not protected by GDPR from this.

US LAW

The Patriot Act

Allows US intelligence agencies to access data from US-based companies without a warrant or any notice to you. Using an American AI means agreeing to this by default.

EU REGULATION

The EU AI Act (2024)

Imposes strict obligations on how AI systems are used in high-risk categories. Non-compliance exposes your company to fines of up to 7% of global turnover. We help you build a full compliance file.
